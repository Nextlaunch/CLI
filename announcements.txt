__**Nextlaunch V2.0.0 (Phoenix Rising)**__
Nextlaunch is an advanced terminal program to provide an easy, low power method for you to keep track of what is going on in the rocket launch community!

Features:
- Power efficient
- Feature rich
- Easy to use
- Easy to understand
- Regular feed updates
- Beautiful, interactive text based UI
- Backed by a community of space nerds!
